FAQ

How do I set up my NeckGuard? ▾

Charge your device for 2 hours, install the CaliCare App, and pair via Bluetooth.

Is there a mobile app? ▾

Yes! Download the CaliCare App on Android or iOS to monitor your device.

Can I export my health data? ▾

Absolutely. You can export data as PDF or CSV from the app dashboard.

Does it work offline? ▾

Yes. Data syncs locally and updates online when a connection is available.

How often should I wear it? ▾

Office workers: 4-6 hrs/day, Sleep tracking: full night, Recovery patients: As prescribed

Can I shower with it? ▾

❌ No - IP54 splash-resistant only

How does sleep tracking work?▾

3D motion sensors detect: Head position, Neck angle, Position changes

Why won't my device connect? ▾

YTry: Restart Bluetooth, Reboot device, Update App

Vibration alerts stopped working ▾

Check battery (under 10% disables alerts), Verify alert settings

Is it safe post-surgery? ▾

Consult surgeon first - vibrations may affect: Spinal implants, Recent fusions, Healing tissues
